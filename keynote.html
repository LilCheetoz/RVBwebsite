The Speaker

Dr. Rob Bogosian

Dr. Rob Bogosian is the founder of RVB Associates and one of the foremost voices on leadership, organizational culture, and the dynamics of voice and silence in the workplace. As a researcher, author, and consultant who has worked with Fortune 500 companies and global organizations, he brings an unparalleled depth of knowledge to every stage.

Rob's presentations are not motivational speeches — they are evidence-based, intellectually rigorous, and practically grounded. Audiences leave not just inspired but equipped: with frameworks, tools, and a new way of seeing their role as leaders.

He is the author of Breaking Corporate Silence and The Seven Myths of Leadership in Business, and draws from his original research throughout his presentations to ground every insight in data, not opinion.

Breaking Corporate Silence: How Leaders Create Cultures of Voice

Leadership Keynote

The Seven Myths of Leadership in Business

Based on his latest book, Dr. Bogosian dismantles the most common — and most damaging — leadership myths that prevent managers from reaching their full potential. A revelatory talk for leaders at every level who want to move beyond conventional wisdom and lead with genuine effectiveness.

Culture Keynote

Culture Isn't What You Say — It's What You Tolerate

An unflinching look at how organizational culture is actually formed — not through values posters or mission statements, but through the behaviors leaders consistently accept, ignore, or reward. A provocative and actionable keynote for leaders responsible for shaping their organization's culture.

Explores the intersection of organizational silence and business performance, showing how the things leaders and employees don't say are costing organizations in engagement, innovation, and results — and what to do about it. Ideal for executive and senior leadership audiences.
