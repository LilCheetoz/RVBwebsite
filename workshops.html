Why Our Workshops Work

Data-Driven. Human-Centered.
Results-Focused.

Every RVB Associates workshop begins with data. Before anyone sets foot in the room, we've analyzed your organization's SVI (Silence & Voice Inventory) diagnostic results to understand your culture's unique profile — where voice is strong, where silence takes hold, and what's driving the gap.

This means your workshop isn't a generic training program — it's a precision-designed experience built around your specific challenges, your team dynamics, and your goals. Participants leave with insights and tools they can use immediately.

  • Grounded in your SVI diagnostic data, not generic content
  • Delivered onsite by experienced RVB Associates consultants
  • Customized for your industry, team size, and culture
  • Followed by action planning and accountability structures
  • Linked to measurable outcomes and behavioral change

Our Process

How We Design Your Workshop

Every workshop follows a rigorous design process to ensure your investment delivers maximum impact.

1

Discovery Call

We start with a conversation to understand your organization's current state, goals, team dynamics, and desired outcomes from the workshop experience.

2

SVI Assessment

Participants complete the Silence & Voice Inventory. We analyze the aggregate data to create a culture profile specific to your team or organization.

3

Custom Design

Using your SVI results and discovery insights, we design a workshop curriculum that addresses your specific culture gaps and leadership development needs.

4

Facilitated Delivery

An experienced RVB Associates consultant facilitates your workshop onsite, creating a safe, engaging, and challenging learning environment.

5

Action Planning & Follow-Through

Every workshop concludes with individual and team action commitments. We provide follow-up support to maintain momentum and track progress.
