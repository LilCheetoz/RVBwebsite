The Core Problem We Solve

Why Leaders & Organizations Underperform

Most organizations struggle not because their people lack talent or intelligence — but because their culture prevents honest, open communication. When employees and leaders stay silent instead of speaking up, organizations drift toward mediocrity, missed opportunities, and avoidable crises.

At RVB Associates, we go beneath the surface. We identify the root causes of leadership performance gaps and the hidden cultural forces — the Culture of Silence — that hold your organization back. Then we help you build a Culture of Voice where candor, courage, and collaboration thrive.

  • Diagnose leadership performance gaps at every level
  • Identify the organizational silence patterns holding you back
  • Design and implement culture transformation programs
  • Build internal leadership capability that sustains change
The Fundamental Distinction

Culture of Voice vs. Culture of Silence

The difference between these two cultures determines whether your organization reaches its potential — or falls short of it.

Culture of Voice

  • Leaders actively invite dissent and alternative viewpoints
  • Employees feel safe raising concerns and new ideas
  • Candid feedback flows upward, downward, and laterally
  • Problems are surfaced early before they become crises
  • High engagement, trust, and psychological safety
  • Decisions are informed by diverse perspectives
  • Innovation and continuous improvement flourish
  • Accountability is shared across the organization

Culture of Silence

  • Leaders surround themselves with "yes" people
  • Employees withhold concerns out of fear or futility
  • Critical information never reaches decision-makers
  • Problems fester undetected until they become crises
  • Low engagement, high turnover, and distrust
  • Groupthink drives poor, unilateral decisions
  • Stagnation and missed opportunities accumulate
  • Blame culture undermines performance and morale
Our Proprietary Tool

The SVI Diagnostic

The Silence & Voice Inventory (SVI) is RVB Associates' proprietary diagnostic tool, developed through years of original research. It measures the health of your organizational culture along the voice-silence spectrum, pinpointing exactly where — and why — silence takes hold.

The SVI provides a quantitative, data-driven baseline for your culture transformation. Every workshop, coaching engagement, and organizational program we design is grounded in your SVI results.

  • Validated research instrument with proven reliability
  • Benchmarked against thousands of leaders and organizations
  • Results delivered in clear, actionable reports
  • Forms the data foundation for every engagement
