About RVB Associates

We Build Cultures
Where Leaders Thrive

RVB Associates is a leadership development and organizational consulting firm with a singular focus: helping executives and their organizations reach their full potential through intentional culture shaping.

We are the authors of Breaking Corporate Silence — the first extensively researched book on how leaders create Cultures of Voice and avoid the dangers of organizational silence.

  • Diagnose root causes of leadership performance gaps
  • Build Cultures of Voice that drive engagement and results
  • Develop high-performing executive teams and leaders
  • Deliver evidence-based, sustainable organizational change
