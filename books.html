Breaking Corporate Silence

How High-Influence Leaders Create Cultures of Voice
By Rob Bogosian & Christine Mockler Casper
★★★★★
Corporate Culture Leadership Communication

Breaking Corporate Silence is the first extensively researched book that examines the causes and consequences of silence and voice in organizations. It provides a compelling framework for understanding why people stay silent at work — and what high-influence leaders can do about it.

Based on years of original research and real-world consulting experience, this book gives leaders the tools to diagnose their organizational culture, understand their own role in perpetuating silence, and take concrete steps to build a Culture of Voice — where candor, courage, and collaboration drive performance.

The Seven Myths of Leadership in Business

Moving Beyond Platitudes to Lead Effectively
By Dr. Rob Bogosian
★★★★★
Leadership Management Development

In today's complex, rapidly changing business environment, leaders need more than inspiration and platitudes — they need clarity, rigor, and real insight. Dr. Rob Bogosian challenges the conventional wisdom that has long shaped how organizations select, develop, and evaluate leaders.

Drawing on decades of research and consulting experience, he identifies seven pervasive myths that lead well-intentioned managers astray — and replaces each with a more honest, effective, and evidence-based framework for leadership. This book gives managers the information they need to lead in a complex world where platitudes have a minimal place.
