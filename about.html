About RVB Associates

Aligning Business Strategy
with Leadership Excellence

We are a leadership development and organizational consulting firm dedicated to aligning business development strategies with leadership development goals and corporate culture.

Our Mission

Helping Leaders
Realize Their Potential

RVB Associates was founded on a simple but powerful belief: that when leaders reach their full potential, their organizations follow. We partner deeply with our clients — understanding their unique context, challenges, and goals — to design and deliver transformative experiences that last.

At the core of our work is a focus on Cultures of Voice — the kind of open, honest, and courageous organizational environments where leaders and teams can perform at their best. We diagnose, design, and sustain these cultures through research-backed methodology and genuine human partnership.

  • Grounded in two decades of original research
  • Practitioners who have led organizations themselves
  • Tailored to every client's unique context
  • Focused on sustainable, measurable outcomes
What Guides Us

Our Core Values

The principles that inform everything we do — every engagement, every conversation, every recommendation.

Research-Backed Rigor

Every framework and tool we use is grounded in empirical research, including our own original studies on voice and silence in organizations.

Genuine Partnership

We don't just deliver services — we build relationships. We invest deeply in understanding your context before recommending a path forward.

Courage & Candor

We tell leaders what they need to hear, not just what they want to hear. Real development requires honest, courageous conversations.

Sustainable Change

We design for the long term. Our goal is to build internal capability so that organizations continue to grow and improve after our engagement ends.

Human-Centered

We believe the quality of an organization is ultimately the quality of its people and the quality of the relationships between them.

Results-Driven

Everything we do is tied to measurable business outcomes. Leadership development is not a soft initiative — it drives real performance.

The People Behind RVB

Meet the Team

Experienced practitioners, researchers, and coaches committed to your organization's success.

Founder & Managing Principal

Christine Mockler Casper

Principal & Co-Author

Nancy Brodsky

Mark Soto
